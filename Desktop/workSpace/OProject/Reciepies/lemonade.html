lemonde


Ingredients

  • Freshly squeezed lemons
  • Water
  • Granulated sugar
  • Optional: Ice, lemon slices, or mint for garnish.

  1. Squeeze the Lemons: Start by squeezing fresh lemons to extract their tangy juice. Adjust the quantity based on your taste preference.
  2. Prepare Simple Syrup (Optional): In a separate pot, you can create a simple syrup by dissolving sugar in an equal amount of water over low heat. Allow it to cool before using.
  3. Mix Lemon Juice and Water: In a pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice with water. Adjust the ratio to achieve your preferred level of tartness.
  4. Sweeten to Taste: Gradually add the desired amount of granulated sugar or the prepared simple syrup, adjusting sweetness according to your liking.
  5. Stir Well: Stir the mixture thoroughly to ensure that the lemon juice, water, and sugar are well combined.
  6. Serve Over Ice: Optionally, serve the homemade lemonade over ice to enhance its crisp and refreshing quality.
  7. Garnish (Optional): Garnish the lemonade with a slice of lemon or a sprig of mint for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal.
  8. Enjoy:Pour yourself a glass of this homemade lemonade, kick back, and relish the delightful combination of tartness and sweetness in every sip. Cheers to your refreshing homemade creation!
