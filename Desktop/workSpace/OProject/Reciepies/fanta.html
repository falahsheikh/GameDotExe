Ingredients

  • Freshly squeezed oranges
  • Sparkling water
  • Natural sweetener (such as honey or agave syrup)
  • Optional: Ice, fresh orange slices, or mint for garnish.

Steps

  1. Squeeze the Oranges: Begin by squeezing fresh oranges to extract their vibrant juice. Aim for a quantity that suits your taste preferences.
  2. Mix with Sparkling Water: In a pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed orange juice with sparkling water. Adjust the ratio based on your desired level of effervescence.
  3. Add Natural Sweetener: Incorporate a dash of natural sweetener, such as honey or agave syrup, into the mix. Adjust the sweetness to your liking.
  4. Stir Gently: Gently stir the ingredients together to ensure an even distribution of flavors.
  5. Serve Over Ice: Optionally, serve the Fizztastic Orange Burst over ice to enhance its refreshing appeal.
  6. Garnish (Optional): For a finishing touch, garnish the drink with a slice of fresh orange or a sprig of mint.
  7. Enjoy: Pour yourself a glass of this homemade delight, sit back, and savor the fizzy, fruity goodness of your very own "Fizztastic Orange Burst"!
